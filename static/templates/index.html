About Me

I have a basic UHF/VHF setup with a few HTs, and a portable QRP HF rig. I enjoy going to local parks and hills to see who I can contact. You might find me on 10m SSB or FT8. I constructed a speaker wire dipole that works well on 10m. As a General class licensee, I've been experimenting with other antenna designs on various bands. I've managed to make DX contacts with only 5-10 watts.

My goal is to contact as many states and countries as possible, taking advantage of the peak of Solar Cycle 25. I'm a member of the San Francisco Amateur Radio Club (SFARC), the California Historical Radio Society (CHRS), and the ARRL.

I volunteer as a radio operator (K6JOB) aboard the SS Jeremiah O'Brien for events like Museum Ships On The Air. My brother (KR4AMW) lives in Georgia, giving us a coast-to-coast link.

Formerly known as KO6BCW (Sep 2023 - Jun 2024).
